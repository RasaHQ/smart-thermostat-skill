Q: Why is my thermostat showing offline?
A: This usually means the thermostat has lost connection to your home network. Check that your Wi-Fi is working and try power cycling the thermostat by removing it from the wall for 30 seconds.

Q: Why is my house cold even though the heating is on?
A: Check that your radiators aren't blocked by furniture, that radiator valves are open, and that there's no air trapped in the system (you may need to bleed the radiators).

Q: How often should I service my heating system?
A: Annual servicing is recommended to keep your boiler running efficiently and safely. This also helps catch small problems before they become expensive repairs.

Q: Why does my heating turn off before reaching the target temperature?
A: Smart thermostats often use "optimum start" to reach your target by a specific time while minimizing energy use. The system may also be accounting for residual heat.
